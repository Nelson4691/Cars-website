Model X

The Model x was designed to be a family car with seating for up to 7 people due to its space efficiency. The Model x also has an impressive self-driven capabilities due to the advance technology the vehicle brings. The coolest thing about the Tesla x aside from having self-driving capabilities is that it has falcon doors which are used by the second and third row seats to enter and exit the vehicle

Inside the Hood

The Tesla Model X delivers an acceleration of 0-60 in 6.2 seconds with a top speed of 130mph. This is possible due to its Duel electric motors in the front and rear of the vehicle which produces 193 kw equaling 259hp. The Model x can travel at a range of 295 mile range before needing a recharge.
